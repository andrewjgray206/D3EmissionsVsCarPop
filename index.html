Carbon Emissions versus Australian Vehicle Population

The following visualisation depicts the trend between Carbon Emissions and Motor Vehicle Population in Australia between 2002 and 2014.
Hovering over each point reveals the information relating to each data point, including what year, and its exact information relating to emissions and vehicle in Australia.
An important note: Hybrid vehicle technology became popular and affordable at the turn of the 2010s and beyond.
